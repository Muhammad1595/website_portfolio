WELCOME TO MY E-PORTFOLIO

Covid-19 was declared a pandemic by the World health organization on 11th March 2020. Since then, it has greatly disrupted normal life and caused a change in the lifestyle of people. Offices and schools were shut down, global air travel was restricted, lockdowns were announced. This caused lifestyle changes to everyone across the globe. During the lockdown times, students were attending schools from the comfort of their houses, thereby saving travel daily time of 1-2 hours. I decided to use this precious time saved, to learn new skills. In my E-Portfolio, I have shared some things that I have learnt in the academic year 2020-2021.
